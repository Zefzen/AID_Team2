Overview & Initial Demo

Innovation Idea

We’re developing an AI-powered dashboard solution that predicts exactly when and how industrial machines need maintenance by analyzing real-time data such as machine performance and energy usage. This helps engineers and technicians move beyond inefficient scheduled maintenance, reducing downtime and reliance on traditional methods.

Demo

Usability

We’re developing an AI-powered dashboard for IKEA’s production and logistics facilities that predicts precise maintenance needs using machine data, automating notifications to reduce downtime and manual effort.

Feasibility

We’re building an AI-based system that predicts machine failures, recommends maintenance, and visualizes data through dashboards for managers and technicians, with a prototype refined using IKEA or sample data and user feedback.

Viability

This solution aligns with IKEA’s sustainability and innovation goals by reducing unplanned downtime, waste, and emissions through predictive maintenance. It supports the 2030 climate-positive strategy while improving efficiency, working conditions, and people-centered design.

Sustainability

The solution will reduce emissions, energy use, and waste while extending equipment lifespan and improving worker safety and satisfaction.

Course Information

TEK830 - Team 2 - Predictive Maintenance Innovation

This project is part of the TEK830 - Innovation Project course at Chalmers University of Technology. The course focuses on applying design thinking principles to real-world problems, encouraging creativity, collaboration, and user-centered design.

Our use of AI

During this course we have used AI tools such as ChatGPT and DALL·E to assist in brainstorming ideas, generating content, and creating visual assets. These tools have helped us enhance our creativity and efficiency throughout the project.
