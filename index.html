Overview & Initial Demo

Innovation Idea

We're building an AI-powered application that uses digital twin technology to revolutionize predictive maintenance in industrial settings. Our solution combines machine data, electricity usage, and other factors with powerful AI to pinpoint exactly when and how specific parts need maintenance, then notifies the required personnel.

Problem We're Solving

It's hard for engineers to keep track of when machines need maintenance or repair. Today's regularly scheduled maintenance is incredibly inefficient since 99% of the time it's not strictly necessary at that specific time. There's also over-reliance on experienced workers' knowledge, creating complications with "this is how it's always been done" mentality.

Target Users

Our target users are factory workers and industries dealing with energy consumption oscillations or production deficiencies. We focus on technicians, engineers, and maintenance managers who need better tools to optimize equipment performance.

Prototype Dashboard Coming Soon

We're developing a dual-view interface: a streamlined dashboard for managers and a detailed technical view for engineers and technicians.
